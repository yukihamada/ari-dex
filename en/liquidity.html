Process

How It Works

Four steps to start earning. Each LP position is minted as an ERC-721 NFT that you own and control.

1

Deposit a Token Pair

Select any supported trading pair (e.g., ETH/USDC, WBTC/ETH). Deposit both tokens in the ratio determined by your selected price range.

2

Select Your Price Range (Tick Range)

Choose the price bounds where your liquidity will be active. Narrower ranges earn more fees per dollar but require more active management. The UI provides suggested ranges based on historical volatility.

3

Earn Trading Fees + $ARI Rewards

When trades execute within your price range, you earn a proportional share of trading fees. Additionally, $ARI emission rewards are distributed to active liquidity providers every epoch (7 days).

4

Manage or Compose Your Position

Your LP position is an ERC-721 NFT. Adjust your range, add/remove liquidity, or use it as collateral in other DeFi protocols. Full composability with the broader ecosystem.

Transparency

Risk Disclosure

Providing liquidity carries real risks. We believe LPs deserve full transparency, not just marketing.

⚠ Impermanent Loss

Impermanent loss (IL) is inherent to all AMM liquidity provision. When the price ratio of your deposited tokens changes, your position's value diverges from simply holding. Concentrated liquidity amplifies both fees earned and IL exposure.

Mitigation: ARI's batch auction model eliminates sandwich-attack-driven IL. Historical data from similar mechanisms shows 5-8% reduction in IL compared to traditional AMMs. However, IL from organic price movement remains.

⚠ Smart Contract Risk

All DeFi protocols carry smart contract risk. Bugs or vulnerabilities could result in partial or total loss of deposited funds.

Mitigation: 3 independent security audits planned (pre-mainnet). $10M bug bounty program. Formal verification of core settlement logic. Timelock on all admin functions. No proxy upgradability on core contracts.

⚠ Active Management Required

Concentrated liquidity positions require active monitoring. If the market price moves outside your selected range, your position stops earning fees entirely and you hold 100% of the less-valuable token.

Mitigation: ARI will provide automated range-adjustment vaults (post-launch). The UI includes price alerts and range health indicators. Full-range positions remain available for passive LPs (lower capital efficiency).

⚠ Protocol Risk

ARI is under active development (Phase 0). The protocol has not launched yet. Token economics, fee structures, and boost mechanics described on this page represent the current design and may change before mainnet.

All changes to finalized parameters will go through governance vote by veARI holders post-launch.
