03 / Our Edge

Structural advantage, not a feature

ARI doesn't patch existing AMMs. It replaces the vulnerable architecture entirely.

🛡

Intent + Encrypted Mempool + Batch Auction

Users express what they want, not how. Orders are encrypted until batch execution. No frontrunning is possible -- it's a protocol-level guarantee, not an add-on.

🌐

Cross-Chain Abstraction

One intent, any chain. Solvers compete to fill cross-chain orders. Users never touch a bridge, pick an RPC, or worry about gas tokens.

🤖

AI-Native Development

2 founders + AI agents, not 50 engineers. AI-driven architecture, spec generation, and code. 10x capital efficiency from day one.

ARI = Arithmetic of Intents

The name captures the core idea: trading reduced to pure mathematical optimization of user intents. No orderbook manipulation, no mempool sniping, no wasted gas. Just math.
